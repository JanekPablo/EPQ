Initial Aim:

Light Meter- Artefact

To produce a piece of software that receives the Light Intensity Value for the specific location, where your shooting, and converts this information into camera details for the Photographer to use: Shutter Speed, Aperture, ND Filter.  This will initially take the form of a Pc run piece of software, however, if time allows I will convert the project into an Android App to allow for more functionality and ease of use. The Light Sensor used will be connected to a Raspberry Pi which in turn will be able to connect to the software coded this allows me to focus on the programming rather than the electricals behind actually making a light meter. Hopefully, the results in an accurate reading of light intensity inputted into the software.
